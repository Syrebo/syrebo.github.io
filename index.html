How to Measure

Two quick measurements

Grab a ruler or tape measure. Measure your dominant rehabilitation hand flat and relaxed for the most accurate result.

1

Hand Length

Measure vertically from the tip of the middle finger straight down to the wrist crease.

2

Hand Width

Measure across the widest part of the palm, at the knuckles of the metacarpal joints.
